From Our Home to Yours

We are a young Spanish team, and these are the recipes we grew up with — the ones cooked at home, passed down without measurements, learned by watching and helping in the kitchen. In Helsinki, we share them exactly as we know them: simple, honest, and meant to be enjoyed together

We cook the way we learned at home: calmly, with care, and without rushing. We take time to explain, to answer questions, and to share the small details that make each recipe special. It feels less like a class, and more like cooking together.

Our Unique Fiestas

A culinary, participatory, and cultural experience available only from January 18th to February 13th.

Spanish Home Cooking

3 Hours | All Levels

This is our most representative Fiesta. Take a culinary tour across Spain and taste the most iconic dishes—from Galicia to the Canary Islands. Try the best Iberian ham (better than Italian!), the famous tortilla de patatas, and the incredible Valencian paella. Cook, taste, and enjoy just like at a real Spanish table!

  • Duration: 3h
  • Price: 88€·VAT INCLUDED

Churros, Torrijas & Crema Catalana

2 Hours | All Levels

If you love sweets and coffee breaks are your favorite moment, this Fiesta is for you! Don’t miss the chance to try authentic churros with chocolate, the traditional Easter torrija, and the amazing Crema Catalana with a crispy caramelized top.

Basque Pintxos & Asturian Cachopo

3.5 Hours | All Levels

Our most famous Fiesta! Learn to make the prettiest and most delicious pintxos, just like in Bilbao, and enjoy an incredible cachopo filled with authentic Iberian ham and Cabrales cheese. Fun, hands-on, and full of northern Spanish flavor.

Connection to the Land

Experience Spain through its flavors, stories, and people.

Real Stories

Learn the stories behind every ingredient and recipe, passed down from our families and our kitchens. Taste Spain’s history as you cook.

Authentic Ingredients

We use the finest Spanish ingredients—olive oil, saffron, Iberian ham, and regional cheeses—to bring the true taste of Spain straight to your plate. Every bite reflects the flavors of our kitchens and our land.

Language Immersion

Practice Spanish naturally while cooking, learning the words and expressions locals use every day in a relaxed and friendly environment.

Building Community

A place to connect, share, and laugh—where Spanish food becomes the bridge between cultures and people.
